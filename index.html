Log Vehicles Mileage

Press the keyboard keys that repersent the desired function

"W" = Drive to work, increase mileage by 36.8km ( the KM it takes to drive to and from work)

"B" = Drive to boyfriends house, increase mileage by 312km (the distance to and from my boyfriends house)

"H" = Car honks!

"S" = Rewrites the stats about the car and lets you know if an oil change is required

"T" = Got an oil change!
